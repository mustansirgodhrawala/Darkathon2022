Market list

Markets in Tor network offers various things. Not always legal. So be careful, you can end up in jail for illegal products when you don't take care of your operational security. Read recommended guides to be sure you are doing it correctly.

Always buy from trusted sellers with good reputation, always use escrow, do not hold cryptocurrency/founds on the market wallet longer than the duration of the transaction and before you buy something read news about markets, maybe one of them is actually an exit scam or preparing to be an exit scam! By following these four simple rules, you reduce the likelihood of being cheated. If someone urges you to omission escrow, know that they are most likely planning to rob and cheat you.

We also provide referral links to the markets. If you want to support our work, register using " ref link " It cost you nothing and we can gain some extra coins to cover server maintenance. Thanks!

If market or shop from the list below have a Sub Dread or Recon link, please visit it and read opinions, comments and other information before you buy. Markets with public and external community are more trusted and if one of them is going to be exit scam, you will get that info really fast in compare to the one not having public form of communication. We are updating market links ASAP, but our actions are also based on publicly available information and can be delayed.
