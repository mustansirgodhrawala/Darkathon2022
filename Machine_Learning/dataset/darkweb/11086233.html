Can I freely dispose of withdrawals and deposits without a third party?

Yes, you are the controller of the withdrawal and deposit. No person can even if one of the team controls your account, withdrawal or deposit, all options are you the only one in control.

How do I create a ESCROW deal?

You must create a ESCROW through your account and send a request to the seller's account, who must also have an account on the platform, free of charge, without the need to write to us

How do I withdraw my balance as a seller?

After completing the deal  with the buyer, the balance will be in your account immediately, and you withdraw the amount quickly to the address of your own wallet

Do you accept deposits externally from the platform?

We do not ask you to pay by e-mail or an external method. You are the only controller of the withdrawal and deposit through your account

I am a seller, do I need an order to open an SELLER account?

No, the platform works in an orderly manner. All you have to do is register an account on the platform and send your username to the buyer. The buyer must also have an account on the platform.
