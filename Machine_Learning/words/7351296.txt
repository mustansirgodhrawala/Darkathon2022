    Credit Cards Manual
    What can you do with the Credit Cards we sell?

      There are several options:

    • Cash out money from the Card.
	  • With Card Holder's personal info you can buy products in online stores.
	  • You can buy products in regular stores because all cards come with PIN-code.
	  • Link Card to PayPal account and Spend Money.
	  • Enroll online Banking Account and Send Money online or exchange to another Payment System.
	  • You can also come up with other options...
    WHEN YOU CASHING OUT MONEY FROM A CARD, DON'T FORGET ABOUT YOUR SAFETY:

	  • Don't show your face, each ATM has few cameras.
	  • Don't Cash Out very Large Amount or more than one Card at one ATM.
	  • When you Cashing Out Money from ATM, acting like it's your own Card you use.
